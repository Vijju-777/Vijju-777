### ⚡ Built. Tested. Shipped. — in 24 hours.

**CyberShield AI** is a real-time illegal betting & gambling website detector built specifically for India, with multilingual support. Paste any URL → the engine cross-references it against 200+ known illegal platforms, scans for risk signals, and maps findings directly to Indian law.

**Detection Signals:**
- 🔍 Betting/gambling keyword detection in domains
- 🌐 Suspicious TLD analysis (`.online`, `.club`, `.xyz`)
- 🇮🇳 India-targeted domain pattern recognition
- 🧒 Youth-targeting indicator scanning
- 📊 Live Gambling Risk Score + Trust Score (0–100)
- ⚖️ Legal status mapping across **4 Indian Acts**

**Legal Frameworks Mapped:**
| Act | Coverage |
|---|---|
| 🏛️ Public Gambling Act, 1867 | Core gambling legality |
| 💻 IT Act 2000 — Section 67 | Cyber obscenity / digital offences |
| 🧒 Minor Protection (Under 18) | Youth risk safeguards |
| 💱 FEMA — Foreign Platforms | Cross-border betting flows |

**Live stats from the build:** `2,847` sites flagged in testing • `94.2%` detection accuracy • one-click **Report to Cyber Crime India** action for flagged domains.
